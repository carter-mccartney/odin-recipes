Lasagna

Credit: Dotdash Meredith Food Studios

Description

Lasagna is a high carb, family-friendly, cheese-loving, pasta-powering dish meal. This made up recipe will surely start your cooking dreams off with a lovely homemade favorite.

Ingredients

  • ground beef (2 lb)
  • italian sausage (1 lb)
  • minced onion (1-2 onions)
  • garlic (2 cloves)
  • crushed tomatoes (1 can 28 oz)
  • tomato sauce (2 cans 6.5 oz)
  • water (1/2 cup)
  • dried basil leaves (1.5 tsp)
  • salt (1.5 tsp)
  • italian seasoning (1 tsp)
  • ground pepper (1/4 tsp)
  • lasagna noodles (12 large noodles)
  • ricotta cheese (16 oz)
  • egg (1 egg)
  • mozzarella cheese (3/4 cup)
  • Parmesean cheese (3/4 cup)

Steps

  1. Cook the sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat in a large saucepan until well browned.
  2. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, and water. Season with pepper. Cover it and simmer for about 1.5 hrs, stirring occasionally.
  3. In another pan, bring water to a boil. We are cooking the lasagna noodles in this, so make sure there is enough water. Add the lasagna noodles and salt into the water and boil for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain the noodles and rinse them with cold water.
  4. In a mixing bowl, mix the ricotta cheese, egg, parsley, and 1/2 tsp salt.
  5. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F. This step can be done earlier if you are a speedy-petey.
